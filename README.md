# stickman-sports
A little stickman game, for fitness and for fun.
-------------------------------------------------

This is just a small game I made to entertain myself when I'm bored. 
You control a stickman as he jumps on a trampoline or off a cliff. 
Make him flip, spin, or dive.
(Trampoline still needs some minor graphical improvements)

--------------------------------------------------
Requires an Android phone and is still under development, but feel free to try it out and let me know what you think.
